## features:

_These are temporary. more will be added in the future._

### ESP / "Wallhacks"
See players through walls with nametags, skeleton esp and distance. there is also ESP for AI/NPCs, containing their HP and nametags.

### Boss tracker / Boss status
Displays special npcs hp and if they are alive or not from across the map.

### Aimbot / AI Aimbot
Lock onto players (and AI) heads with legit smoothing.

### Viewmodel Changer
Change viewmodel to forcefield textures, removing surface appearances of your viewmodel. adds extra visibility and is toggleable off.

### Inventory checker GUI/ESP
Displays players items either on ESP or as a GUI, showing all of their items and which part of their clothes itâ€™s stored in.

### Keybinds / List
Displays keybinds as a UI. presets are made.

### Overlayallergy
Deletes all (mostly altyn) overlays. also covers NVG overlays, granting more visibility.

### ESP Tracers
shows tracers from the bottom of your screen to all players. lowers visibility but allows you to see everyone even off your screen.

### Shadowless
Deletes all shadows, brightening the game and boosting performances.

### Corpse / Item ESP
Dropped items and corpses are highlighted with nametags and BoxHandleAdornments for performance.
